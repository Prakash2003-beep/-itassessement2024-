Top Travel Routes & Places in Nepal

Discover the beauty, culture, and adventure of Nepal

Pokhara

A beautiful city with lakes, caves, and breathtaking views of the Annapurna mountain range. It is the gateway to the Annapurna Circuit.

Kathmandu

The capital of Nepal filled with ancient temples, rich culture, and UNESCO World Heritage Sites like Swayambhunath and Pashupatinath.

Lumbini

The birthplace of Lord Buddha and an important pilgrimage site. It features beautiful monastic zones and sacred gardens.

Chitwan National Park

Home to wildlife like rhinos, tigers, and elephants. Offers jungle safaris, canoe rides, and local Tharu cultural shows.
